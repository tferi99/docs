=========================
Howto setup pickup group?
=========================
- CUCM/Call Routing/Call Pickup Group/(Add New)
- Add an usused DN as 'Call Pickup Group Number'
- Save
- Go to lines and assign pickup group to phones one by one
	
		[Call Forward and Call Pickup Settings]/Call Pickup Group
		
To check assigned lines call 'Dependecy Records' of pickup group.
