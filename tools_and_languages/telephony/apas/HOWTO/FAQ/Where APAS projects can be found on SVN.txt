========================================
Where APAS projects can be found on SVN?
========================================
You can find SVN directories of projects in Jenkins configuration.

1. Go to Jenkins (http://192.168.150.10:8080/).

2. Choose version (e.g. APAS-Trunk).

3. Here you can find projects. Choose one of them.

4. Call 'Configure' option from left menu.

5. SVN directory:	[Source Code Management]/Repository URL
