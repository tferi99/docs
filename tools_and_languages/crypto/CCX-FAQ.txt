Terminology
===========
Market:		A market is usually a pair of traded crypto/fiat currencies.

Base (or Accounting) Currency/quote currency: 

			The base currency represents how much of the quote currency is needed for you to get one unit of the base currency. 
			For example, if you were looking at the BTC/USD currency pair, the Bitcoin would be the base currency and the U.S. dollar 
			would be the quote currency.
			
			- The base currency as the domestic currency or accounting currency to represent all profits and losses.
			- The quote currency, commonly known as "counter currency," is the second currency in both a direct and indirect currency pair 
				and is used to determine the value of the base currency.
