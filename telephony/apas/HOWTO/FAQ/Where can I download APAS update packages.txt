==========================================
Where can I download APAS update packages?
==========================================

Method 1 - latest:

	SEE:	How to upgrade an ISO APAS to the latest version?
	
	- get update package from /Jenkins/<APAS-Trunk OR version>/apas-<trunk|version>-x86_64
		e.g. APAS-x86_64-583-11040-Update.pkg
		
		
Method 2 - old versions:

	\\ANDTEK\support\Versionen\APAS\APAS-x86_64-<version>-<revision>-Update.pkg
