=====================
Debugging RTP streams
=====================
- To debug RTP streams generated by APAS use:

	http://apas_test.com:8080/andphone/jsp/dev/rtpstreams.jsp 



- You can enable JMF logging in JSP page (APAS restart required)

	Log will be generated here:
	
		/var/log/apas/jmf.log
